About Us

Zomato, founded in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah, started as a restaurant discovery platform in India. Originally named "Foodiebay," it began as an online restaurant directory offering menu information, reviews, and recommendations for dinners. Over time, the platform evolved into a comprehensive food-tech company offering various services.Zomato's expansion has been significant,extending its services beyond India to numerous countries worldwide. Its growth has included acquisitions and diversification into various food-related verticals, making it a prominent player in the global food-tech.Zomato's transformative transcended geographical boundaries, expanding its footprint beyond India to numerous countries globally. This expansion was by strategic acquisitions, collaborations, and a steadfast commitment to adaptability and cultural sensitivity. Through these endeavors, Zomato solidified its position as a prominent player in the global food-tech landscape, catering to the diverse tastes and preferences of a vast and varied audience.
